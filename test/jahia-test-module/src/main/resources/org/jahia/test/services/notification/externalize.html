Acme in Brief

ACME construction, Europe's leading and largest construction group, with more than 40’000 employees around the world

Acme is the main player in Europe and USA, deploying its expertise across the full spectrum of building and infrastructure construction and operating businesses, including property development and road construction.

Founded in 1950, ACME bases its growth on its international successes, the personal development of its collaborator and the acquisition of companies exercising similar activities with strong recurring profits.
